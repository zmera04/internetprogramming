Our Story:

Following his high school graduation, Holden Caulfield decided to go back to New York with a new outlook on life.No longer a catcher in the rye, Holden has joined the onlookers at the cliff of disillusionment. In New York, Holden wanted everyone to feel the confidence, authenticity, independence, and freedom his red hunting hat brought him. Starting with mending Phoebe’s clothes, and even making her some, Holden chose to create his own fashion brand-- Caulfield.


The Designs:

Caufield is a new, revolutionary fashion brand, whose unique designs are none other than Holden Caufield’s. Caufield has just released it's Winter-Spring Collection, and hopes to release a Spring-Summer collection in the very near future.

A theme in Catcher in the Rye, is the walls one puts up to not feel vulnerable, this theme is explored through the use of layers, and baggy clothing. Holden Caulfield uses his previous experiences at Pencey Preparatory School, and New York City to inspire the designs of his collection. Through Caulfield, Holden is using a difficult period from his life, and creating art from it. From the closet to the runway, it is obvious Holden pours love into his work. Holden is authentically himself, and hopes to encourage others to be authentically themselves. Caulfield hopes our designs inspire wearers to do away with their phoniness, and live with confidence and integrity.


Spring-Winter:

Each piece featured in this collection was inspired by either a particular scene, theme, or character in Catcher in the Rye. The progression of this collection follows the Hero’s Quest archetype-- starting in the ordinary world with practical outfits, progressing into the magical world with haute couture-esque outfits, and ending with changed approach towards practical clothing.

Caulfield hopes the clothes from this collection keeps its wearers comfortable, however they may define it. This items in this collection range from baggy to tight and from modest to exposed, each with a unique silhouette. We our collection helps wearers to not care what others have to say, and realize that what matters if what their wear works for them.
